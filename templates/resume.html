Synopsis

AskMyrtle is a chatbot created for the use of business owners in the Grand Strand area. This chatbot provides the user with information filtered specifically for statistics and facilities in the Grand Strand area. From initial beta testing, the UI that was present in the program didn't provide the user with many unique features. Therefore, Daniel Alimiron and I were tasked to develop a new UI that would be give AskMyrtle its own flare and a unique product.
